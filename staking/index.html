Staking

You can stake Dogelon Index tokens as you would do with any other standard *RC-20 token. For example you can add it in a 1:1 ratio on decentralized exchanges (*swap) to collect fees.

Since the total liquidity is still very low you will benefit from higher fees than with standard Dogelon on popular exchanges.

Another benefit in staking Dogelon Index tokens is you don’t have to worry about impermanent loss because the exchange rate is fixed.
